What is Data Analytics?

Data Analytics refers to the process of examining raw data to uncover valuable insights, identify trends, and support decision-making within organizations. It involves various techniques such as statistical analysis, data mining, predictive modeling, and machine learning to transform data into actionable intelligence. Businesses today rely heavily on data analytics to understand customer behavior, improve operations, and gain a competitive edge.

The field of Data Analytics is vast and evolving, encompassing both descriptive and predictive analytics to interpret past data and forecast future outcomes. Whether you're analyzing sales data, customer feedback, or social media metrics, data analytics helps convert complex datasets into meaningful narratives.
